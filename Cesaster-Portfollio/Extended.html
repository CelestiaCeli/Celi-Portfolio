About Me

I am a dedicated honest sociable programmer/artist who learns quick under pressure and ensures to help doing my own work both art/programming wise but also communicating and connecting a team together with communication.

My latest achievement is winning 2nd place in a game jam done in unity C# , I have spent time working on a project called “KitchenSinkGames” written in c++ including Hangman, Memory Matching. I’m working on learning vulkan and love spending time drawing as visually represented to the right.

I specialize in game design; art and programming. Allowing for close connection between the artists and the programmers. I want to ensure quality and maintainability is focused on both ends.

I have spent a portion of life using games as a backbone when no one was there to socialize. I would love to help other socially struggling people in the same way while giving experiences and worlds that stick and make them question things. Video Games are an art form.

I would love to help your team and act as a bridge between art and code to clear up communication and work in both areas being a two-in-one worker!

Does your programming effectively agree with your art in place? It’s one of the most representative things controlled by code.

I'm always open to consideration from professionals, or even any friends who want to contact me!
